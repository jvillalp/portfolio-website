Intro

Hello World! I began my transition when I moved from Chicago to San Jose, CA 2 months ago. I became interested in technology and realized how fast growing the web development world is.

During the first 2 weeks of Lambda, I have already learned HTML & CSS, which include semantic HTML, tags, how to use the command line interface (CLI) and GitHub. It has been no easy feat, but I am looking forward to learning more about HTML/CSS and the different languages and algorithms we will begin to explore as the course moves forward.

I hope to contribute to the world is a positive way and hopefully make people's lives easier.

In the following links you will exlore the current webpages that I have created.

Please feel free to contact me upon looking at the two web pages, "Sweet Bakery" & "Great Idea!" should you have questions or comments.
